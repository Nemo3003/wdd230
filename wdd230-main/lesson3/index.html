Purpose

Chamber of Commerce Kalispell is an influential advocate for Kalispell and Northwest Montana by providing quality programs and services that drive the economic prosperity and sustainability of our community.

The Kalispell Chamber of Commerce is a regional leader in cultivating a robust economy so Northwest Montana's businesses, community, and people will grow and thrive.

We value excellence in enterprise, commitment to progress, stewardship of our natural resources and Montana lifestyle, ethical Conduct
